About Easy Shop Malta

For Shop Owners

Easy Shop Malta is a platform which allows shops from around Malta and Gozo to sell items their products online.

Where regular shops might only facilitate sales during office hours, Easy Shop allows shops sell their merchandise 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, regarless if their physical shop is open or not.

This means that as a shop owner, you will be able to capture more sales, boost revenue, whilst not having to pay additional employees for expanded opening hours.

To start selling on Easy Shop, please apply here.

For Buyers

Easy Shop allows you to purchase items from your favorite local outlets. Shop hassel free online, avoid traffic, parking, queues and ultimately save yourself time and money!

As the listed products are only from Maltese & Gozitan shops, you can buy knowing that your product comes from a reputable local shop, a feature that is seldom available when shopping online.

Ordering products on Easy Shop is explained further in the How It Works Section.

Secure Checkout

For Shop Owners

In order to receive payment, shop owners must have a registered payment method. This is required to facilitate seamless transfer of funds from buyers to your account. Errors in your payment information will result in a broken transaction.

What Bank Information Is Needed?

The standard account identifier would be required. Please note that Easy Shop does not ask for sensitive bank information.

Please do not provide any sensitive bank information to us, or others online.

For Buyers

Our checkout is powered by Stripe, an international giant in online payment processing. Although you may save your payment information we would ask you to remain vigilent with your bank details online.

What Bank Information Is Needed To Checkout?

To complete checkout, a card number, cvv and expiry are needed. We will soon add other payment options, via Apple pay and others.
