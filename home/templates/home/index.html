About Giburoo

Maltese Origins

Giburoo is an online platform that enables both Maltese and Gozitan businesses sell their products online.

We aim to provide a platform for shoppers across the Maltese islands gain access to their favorite shops right from their computer, tablet or handheld device.

What's With The Name?

As our business is centered on the Maltese islands and the idea of shopping locally, our name derives from the Maltese words "Ġib" & "Ġibr", meaning "Get It & Collect".

Security & Checkout

For Shop Owners

In order to receive payment, shop owners must have a registered payment method. This is required to facilitate seamless transfer of funds from buyers to your account. Errors in your payment information will result in a broken transaction.

What Bank Information Is Needed?

The standard account identifier would be required. Please note that Easy Shop does not ask for sensitive bank information.

Please do not provide any sensitive bank information to us, or others online.

For Shoppers

Our checkout is powered by Stripe, an international giant in online payment processing. Although you may save your payment information we would ask you to remain vigilent with your bank details online.

What Bank Information Is Needed To Checkout?

To complete checkout, a card number, cvv and expiry are needed. We will soon add other payment options, via Apple pay and others.

How It Works?

For Shop Owners

Firstly, to sell your products on this website, you must first get approval. This process is free, and is our way of ensuring the only products on this website are in fact sourced from legitimate businesses. We do not charge for hosting fees, listing fees or subscription. This service is free to use, and we are available 24 hours a day to help you list your products on our website.

How To Sign Up?

The first step is registering for an account. To do that, please use the link at the bottom of this section. Once we see that you have a legitimate business in Malta and / or Gozo, your account will be activated and you will be given website privilages that will allow you to create & manage your products, view orders, view previous orders and more.
